Do games require installs?

No downloads or installations needed. Every game runs directly in your browser—click and play instantly. We use modern web technologies to deliver smooth gameplay without any setup hassle.

How are new games chosen?

Our team curates based on gameplay quality, performance, and player feedback. We test each title for browser compatibility and fun factor before adding it to the collection. Fresh games are added weekly.

What devices are supported?

Desktop, laptop, tablet, and most modern smartphones work perfectly. We optimize for Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. Older devices may experience slower performance on graphics-intensive titles.

Why does the site feel curated if it's huge?

We organize 10,000+ games into smart categories and featured collections. The search and filter system helps you discover exactly what you want, while our editorial picks highlight the best online gaming experiences daily.

Are all games truly unblocked?

Yes. Every game is browser-based and accessible from school or work networks that typically block gaming sites. No proxy or VPN needed—just open the site and start playing.

Can I save my progress?

Many games support local browser storage to save your progress automatically. For the best experience, use the same browser and device. Account-based saves are coming soon for cross-device sync.
